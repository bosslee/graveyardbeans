About Graveyard Bean

If a coffee tree is to grow well, it needs more than anything else a soil off good structure, permeable and deep. The coffee tree has tap roots. The tap root goes straight down into the soil. The branch roots go down very deep.

The soil must have a good structure so that the roots can penetrate well. All these variables are all possible at the graveyard.

Right in Lim Chu Kang Cementary, we found such a place and Graveyard Bean was born.

Limited Graveyard Bean Available

Due to the small land we have, only 100 packets of Graveyard Bean were produced. Each packet is 500g. There will not be a second production.
